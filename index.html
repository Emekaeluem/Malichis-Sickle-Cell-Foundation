We Are More Than a Foundation

Malichis Sickle Cell Foundation

Malichis Sickle Cell Foundation is a non-governmental organization based in Lagos, Nigeria dedicated to improving the lives of individuals living with sickle cell disorder. For over four years we have remained a trusted source of hope through medical support, awareness campaigns, advocacy and digital empowerment.

Trusted support for sickle cell warriors

We provide medication assistance, counseling, emergency support and programs that empower individuals and families.
