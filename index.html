Who We Are

We Are More Than a Foundation

Malichis Sickle Cell Foundation is a dedicated non-governmental organization based in Lagos, Nigeria committed to improving the lives of individuals living with sickle cell disorder.

For over four years, we have remained a trusted source of hope by providing medical support, raising awareness, advocating for better healthcare, and empowering sickle cell warriors with modern digital skills that create opportunities for financial independence.

Our vision is a society where every sickle cell warrior has access to quality care, acceptance, and the opportunity to thrive.

Our Mission

Holistic Support, Built Around Every Warrior
